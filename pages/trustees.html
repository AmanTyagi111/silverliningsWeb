Our Trustees



Preeti Monga is the Founder CEO & Trustee of the Silver Linings Trust which, offers a host of services to corporations, people with disabilities, women and the citizen at large.

Preeti's, 25 years of professional experience encompasses, business development, Marketing, public relations, patient relations, corporate trainings, motivation speaking, fund raising, writing and counseling. Preeti is the first visually impaired Aerobic instructor in the country, and an outstanding Public Speaker. Her autobiography named ‘The Other Senses’ has been published by Roli Books. Some of her awards include, The National Award 2013 by The President of India, the ‘Red and White Bravery’ Award, the ‘Rajeev Gandhi Manav Seva’ Award and the IIPM ‘Indomitable Spirit of Survival’ award.


Ms. Kapila Vatsyayan is a leading scholar of classical Indian dance, Indian art and Indian architecture and art historian. She was the founding director of the Indira Gandhi National Centre for the Arts, Delhi, and continues as its chairperson. She is also the chairperson of Asia Project, of the India International Center Delhi.

In 1970, she was awarded the Sangeet Natak Academy Fellowship, the highest honor conferred by the Sangeet Natak Academy, India's National Academy for Music, Dance and Drama. In 2011, she was a recipient of the Padma Vibhushan, India's second highest civilian honor, awarded by the Government of India.

She is the author of many books including The Square and the Circle of Indian Arts, Bharata: The Natya Sastra, and Matralaksanam.


Prof. S. Sampath comes with a rich professional experience of over 27 years. He is a Masters in Commerce; MBA specializing in Finance, with a LLB Degree plus a Post Graduate Diploma in Ecology & Environment, M.Phil in Commerce and has been awarded PhD by Mumbai University in Taxation! Prof. Sampath is a practicing Chartered Accountant as well as visiting faculty in a vast number of reputed business schools.

Apart from being professionally associated with, and providing expert consultancy to leading public and corporate sector organizations, Prof. Sampath was selected to represent and appear, before Standing Committee on Finance Set Up of the Parliament to express views on direct taxes, laws amendment bill, and also suggestions for rationalization of taxes.


Mr. Sunil Mittal comes from a family of Freedom Fighters, from Meerut U.P. He graduated from Delhi University, with a B.Sc. & LLB degree! Mr. Mittal has behind him, a rich 35 year of Legal Practice experience, specializing in Family Law as well as all other Civil Law matters. Ever since, his early Law School days, Mr. Mittal has been a social activist, leading the 1977-1978 Jayaprakash Narayan Student's Movement, as Vice President, at Delhi University; and was in addition detained for supporting the same in 1975, while in the first year of his LLB degree course!

Mr. Sunil Mittal has also been closely associated with various Non-Governmental organizations like Saheli, Shakti Shalini and AGG; working towards and actively supporting, the cause of Women and HIV Aids!
